About

I am a high school student at Shiv Nadar School Faridabad, a computer science enthusiast as well as an open-source developer. I have been involved in various open-source projects for the past several years, such as developing an Android-based operating system, creating Android device-specific frameworks, building Alexa skills and web apps, and more. My GitHub profile has more of my work and contributions to the open-source community. As the founder of ShapeShiftOS, I've earned recognition as an XDA Recognized Developer, and have mentored other developers and ensured compliance with open-source licenses.

My passion for open-source development is matched by my interest in natural language processing (NLP) and linguistics, which I have explored through attending the Indian Linguistics Olympiad Camp, designing interaction models for Alexa skills, and studying constructed languages on ConWorkShop and other forums on the Internet.

I have also excelled in various Olympiads, both national and international, in subjects such as mathematics, linguistics, and chemistry, with several notable awards such as representing India at the Asia Pacific Linguistics Olympiad (APLO) and the SOF Academic Excellence Scholarship for being among the top 0.005% of students who appeared for the SOF Olympiads in 2020-21.

I have been actively involved in Model United Nations (MUN) conferences, both as a delegate and as a Secretariat member. I have held leadership positions such as the MUN Secretary of the School Council twice, the Secretary General of the Shiv Nadar School Faridabad MUN 2023, the Head of Delegate Affairs for the Shiv Nadar School Faridabad MUN 2022, and have received commendations at 7 MUNs.
